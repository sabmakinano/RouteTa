The Project

What is RouteTa?

Our Mission

RouteTa is a free, easy-to-use jeepney route finder built for the people of Cebu. We know how confusing public transport can be especially for students, visitors, and everyday commuters trying to get from point A to B. RouteTa makes it simple: just enter where you're coming from and where you're going, and we'll find your route.

Why We Built This

Cebu's jeepney network is one of the most used forms of transport in the city, yet there's very little accessible, digital info about routes. We built RouteTa to bridge that gap no ads, no sign-up, no hassle. Just routes, fast.

How It Works

RouteTa covers jeepney routes across Cebu City and nearby areas including Talisay and Mandaue. Simply type your starting point and destination into the search bar on the home page, and RouteTa will show you the correct jeepney route, including the route code and key stops along the way.
